Forest Gump Adventure

On January 1 2024, I set out on a human-powered journey from my front door in Seattle.

My goal is to live each second fully in the present and ski some epic lines along the way.

I'm running with a baby jogger holding my camping gear, food, and skis.

This adventure was inspired in large part by my friend Matt Montavon who ran 1,100 miles across Alaska with a stroller.

You can stay up to date with the adventure by reading my daily blog Splattern

You can support the adventure by donating to my fundraiser for GiveDirectly.
